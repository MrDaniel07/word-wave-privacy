Privacy Policy for Word Wave
Last updated: February 2026
Word Wave is an educational and productivity application that uses gamified features such as quizzes, streak counters, reminders, and animations to enhance learning and engagement. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Word Wave mobile application.

Information Collection and Use
Word Wave does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or user IDs.
However, the app may collect non-personal information automatically to support functionality, analytics, and advertising. This includes:
* App usage data
* Quiz interactions and streak-related activity
* App preferences and settings
* Device and system information (such as operating system version)
* Crash reports and performance diagnostics
* Advertising-related identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID)
This information does not directly identify individual users.

Data Storage
Word Wave stores and retrieves non-personal app data using cloud services.Stored data is limited to app functionality, progress tracking, and performance improvement purposes.

Purpose of Data Collection
Non-personal data collected by Word Wave is used to:
* Enable app features such as quizzes, streak tracking, and reminders
* Analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality
* Monitor app performance and fix errors
* Display relevant advertisements
* Improve overall user experience

App Permissions
Word Wave may request permission to schedule alarms and send notifications. These permissions are used to:
* Deliver reminders
* Maintain streak counters
* Notify users of quizzes or learning activities
These permissions are not used to collect personal information or to track users outside the app.

Third-Party Services
Word Wave uses third-party services that may collect non-personal information in accordance with their own privacy policies, including:
* Google AdMob (Google Ads)
* Firebase Analytics
* Google Analytics
* Google Play Services
These services may use cookies or similar technologies to provide and improve their services, including personalized or non-personalized advertising.

Advertising
Word Wave displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob and Google Ads.AdMob may use advertising identifiers and other non-personal information to show relevant ads to users.
