Privacy Policy for Word Wave

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Word Wave ("we", "our", or "the app") is a vocabulary learning application designed to help users expand their word knowledge through daily words, interactive quizzes, and engaging features. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Word Wave mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address (optional, only if you choose to create an account for cloud sync)
  • Favorites: Words you save to your favorites collection
  • App Preferences: Theme selection, language preference (English, French, Spanish), notification settings, and notification time

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Learning Progress: Quiz completion data, quiz scores, and streak information
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, words viewed, and general app interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and language settings
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and performance data to improve app stability
  • Advertising ID: Google Advertising ID (for free users to display relevant ads)

1.3 Purchase Information

  • Subscription Status: Pro subscription status (handled securely through Google Play Store)
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history (stored by Google Play, not directly by us)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features including daily word delivery, quiz generation, streak tracking, and favorites management
  • Personalization: To customize your experience with theme preferences, language settings, and notification timing
  • Cloud Sync: To sync your favorites, quiz progress, and streak data across devices (if logged in)
  • Pro Features: To enable premium features (ad-free experience, custom themes, online dictionary) for subscribers
  • Notifications: To send daily word reminders at your preferred time
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app and improve features
  • Advertising (Free Users): To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • App Improvement: To identify bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using the following methods:

  • Local Storage: App preferences, favorites, and basic settings are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
  • Cloud Storage: Quiz progress, streak data, and favorites are backed up to Google Firebase Firestore (optional, requires account)
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
  • Firebase Security: Cloud data is protected by Firebase security rules and authentication

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

4. App Permissions

Word Wave requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required for cloud sync, online dictionary (Pro feature), advertisements, and Firebase services
  • Notifications: To send daily word reminders at your scheduled time
  • Exact Alarm Scheduling: To ensure notifications are delivered at your precise preferred time
  • Billing: To process Pro subscription purchases through Google Play Store

These permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and are not used to collect personal information beyond what is described in this policy.

5. Third-Party Services

Word Wave integrates the following third-party services that may collect data:

5.1 Firebase Services (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: For optional user accounts
  • Cloud Firestore: For cloud data storage and sync
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting and analytics

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

Free users see advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Google Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.3 Google Play Billing

Pro subscriptions are processed through Google Play Store. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play and is not accessible to us.

5.4 Dictionary API

The online dictionary feature (Pro users only) uses the free Dictionary API at dictionaryapi.dev. No personal information is sent to this service - only the word being searched.

Privacy Policy: https://dictionaryapi.dev/

5.5 Google Fonts

The app uses Google Fonts (Inter typeface) for display. Your device may connect to Google's servers to download fonts.

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Advertising and Tracking

Free Users: See advertisements from Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests using the Google Advertising ID.

Pro Subscribers: Enjoy a completely ad-free experience with no advertising tracking.

Opting Out: You can disable personalized ads through your Android device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Firebase/Google services to provide app functionality
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users would be notified)

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the app's services:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account or request data deletion
  • Learning Progress: Retained to track your streaks, quiz history, and favorites
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized, retained for app improvement purposes
  • Crash Logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days
